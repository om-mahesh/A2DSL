Infrastructure

Lab Facilities

State-of-the-art equipment and tools supporting cutting-edge research in hardware security and digital design.

VLSI Design Lab

Advanced EDA tools including Cadence, Synopsys, and Mentor Graphics for RTL design, synthesis, place-and-route, and verification of complex digital systems.

Hardware Security Lab

Specialized equipment for side-channel analysis, fault injection, and physical attack evaluation including power analyzers, EM probes, and glitch injection setups.

FPGA Prototyping

High-end FPGA boards (Xilinx Virtex UltraScale+, Intel Stratix 10) for rapid prototyping of processor architectures, cryptographic accelerators, and SoC designs.

AI/ML Hardware Lab

Dedicated workstations with GPU accelerators for developing and evaluating hardware architectures for machine learning inference and training workloads.

IoT Testbed

Comprehensive IoT development environment with sensor nodes, gateway devices, and cloud integration platforms for edge computing systems.

Research Library

Access to premium journals and conference proceedings to support cutting-edge research and literature reviews.
